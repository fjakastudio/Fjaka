About us

We are Fjaka — an award-winning creative agency in Malmö, Sweden.  

We specialise in creating brand identities, websites, apps, illustration and animations for clients of all kinds. And we do it by taking our clients with us through every step of the design process.

Fjaka was started since we embrace the freedom to define our career paths and reinvent. Collectively, we push each other to be brave, whether we’re sharing ideas or coming up with new ones. 
We all have expertise at something—but no matter the subject, we believe everyone in the room has something valuable to say.

We believe in that invention beats convention

As project partners we help our clients to discover and take ownership of their brand’s unique role in the world.  We don’t work with particular industries, instead we try to work with brands we respect, forging partnerships and treading new ground with each piece of work.

What you can expect

We are visual by heart, and we want to spend less time in meetings and more time communicating our ideas in prototypes or pictures.

With that said, we thought; everybody has a process, so here’s our process ↓

1. Think

In order to solve a problem it needs to be understood. We spend time getting to know your problems, hopes and dreams.

2. Create

In order to achieve an outstanding digital experience we want to create as much as possible during a given phase.

3. Observe

When all said and done, the most important part is to get feedback on the work that's been created.

4. Celebrate

The time has come, we're done and the time for champagne is here. The easiest part is over and now the real work begins.
