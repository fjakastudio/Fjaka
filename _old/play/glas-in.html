Who is Glas In?

Glas In has been manufacturing glass sections in Malmö since 1986. It started with the production of balcony glazing, but as customers' wishes have changed, new products have been added. In 1991, the old premises in the port of Malmö were left for a newly built larger facility in Oxie (just outside Malmö). This facility has been expanded considerably over the years and it is still where Glas Ins' production and head office are located. 
‍
Today, Glas In manufactures everything from uninsulated sections to fully insulated facade sections. Also room sections, glass railings, glass ceilings, aluminum frames etc.

A focus on people

Glas In has a major history and are well established in the southern part of Sweden. It struggles with branching outside of southern Sweden.

Our first task was to really dial in what they wanted to achieve, what are the goals not only for potential customers, but also the different sections of the company. The outcome was several metric driven goals that we based our design on.

With this we designed everything to generate more qualified leads, something Glas In has been struggling with their old website.

A project in collaboration

This project was designed by us at Fjaka, but also our friend at Urban Animal Studio. The development was handeld by Sunfish, who did a great job accommodating us during the design process.
