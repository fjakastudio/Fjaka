How to install the swatches

It's fairly easy to install the swatches, here's a quick guide.

  • Download the swatches.
  • Save the swatches to your Dropbox/iCloud/Google Drive, or e-mail them to yourself.
  • Open your preferred cloud service on your iPad.
  • Click the swatches you want to import.
  • Done.
